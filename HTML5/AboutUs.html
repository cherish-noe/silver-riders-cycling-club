About

Our cycling club is a cycling club based in the North East of England and founded in 1966. Our club is emphasized for the older cyclists, but we arrange for all kinds of cyclists can be participated. The Club is open one evening in a week but in Saturday and Sunday, club is open all day. The club has a calendar of events and there are three different groups to choose from according to a range of abilities of riders. Our club always warmly welcome new riders.
